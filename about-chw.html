Chase Healthcare & Wellness Pvt Ltd is a rapidly growing healthcare company based in India, dedicated to delivering high-quality surgical, pharmaceutical, and wellness solutions across India and global markets.

Our journey began in 2015 with the foundation of OM SAI Pharma, where we served as a trusted super distributor across India, building strong relationships with hospitals, doctors, and healthcare providers.
